PIZZA

Pizza is a traditional Italian dish, originally in the form of a round yeast flatbread baked with a topping of tomato sauce , cheese and often other ingredients such as meat, vegetables, mushrooms and other products. A small pizza is sometimes called pizzetta . A cook who specializes in making pizza is a pizzaiolo .
